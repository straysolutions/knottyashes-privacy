# Privacy Policy

**Proper Sayin's of a Southern Belle** · Last updated: June 2026

Thank you for using Proper Sayin's of a Southern Belle (the "app"). Your privacy matters, and this policy explains, in plain language, what the app does and does not do with information. The short version: the app does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

## Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The app has no accounts, no login, and does not ask for your name, email, location, or contacts.

## Information Stored on Your Device

The app saves a few things locally on your phone so it can remember your choices between visits. This includes your saved favorite sayings, your settings (such as whether the daily notification, clean filter, and haptics are turned on), and a flag noting that you have seen the welcome screen. This information stays on your device, is never sent to us or to anyone else, and is removed if you delete the app.

## Notifications

If you turn on the daily notification, the app schedules reminders locally on your device. These are generated on the phone itself. The app does not use push notifications and does not collect or transmit any device or notification tokens.

## Sharing and Clipboard

When you copy or share a saying, the content is handled by your device's own clipboard and share features. The app does not transmit shared content to us, and we do not see, store, or track what you copy or share.

## Text to Speech

The read-aloud feature uses your device's built-in text-to-speech. No audio or text is sent off the device by the app for this feature.

## Third Parties, Ads, and Analytics

The app contains no advertising, no third-party analytics, and no tracking software. No data is shared with third parties.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. Because the app collects no personal information at all, none is collected from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
